The 4-Step Process Of Our Book Editing And Proofreading Service

Structural Story Editing

This step involves evaluating your story arc and presentation from the highest level. The editor reviews the entire manuscript in detail, evaluating elements like story arc, word count, characters, plot, settings, story flow and pacing, scene openings and endings, consistency, and clarity.

Copyediting

In this stage, the editor makes the manuscript adhere to a style, corrects punctuation and grammar, ensures accuracy and clarity, queries facts or unclear statements, checks that the tone is appropriate for the audience, and checks that references and citations are in order.

Line Editing

This stage of our book editing service focuses on the language and tone of the manuscript. Our professional book editor works with the text to improve its sound and feel, ensuring it is nice to read.

Proofreading

This is the final stage of the process, focusing on minor errors and inconsistencies. Our book proofreading experts ensure that the manuscript is free from spelling, grammar, punctuation, and typographical errors.
